About Me

My coding journey began in June 2019 due to curiosity, I signed up for a 4 week short course with Founders and Coders. It was an introduction to JavaScript and I knew immediately that this was what I wanted to do. The continuous growth and ever changing technology within the industry fills me with such enthusiasm and excitement. Whilst still in my previous employment I started attending meet-ups and completing courses online with Udemy, Free Code Camp and various other sites. Finally in October 2019 I enrolled in a full time Software Engineering course which covered a range of technologies. I graduated from this bootcamp in February 2019 and I still continue to further my knowledge by completing online courses and creating my own projects.
