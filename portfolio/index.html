About Me

Hi, I'm Sarah. I'm learning web development, with the goals of one day becoming an inspiring software engineer in a thriving team environment. I strong passion for creative design and love hacking on ideas and building stuff in my free time.

First, a bit about me. I left home at 17, without understanding what I wanted to be or know how to get there. I managed to get into a few different types of roles in Accounting, Customer Services, Information Management and Construction. In each role I became the person whose colleagues would rely on for software or program training/development. I was able to pick things up very quickly and learn all the functions and short cuts. I used various Microsoft packages such as Excel, Word, Projects, Visio etc. I also used Sage Line 50, Sage Payroll, AutoCad and Oracles RS-SQL but I wanted to know how these programs works and how they were made. Throughout my career, I've often felt unfulfilled in my role and wanting to do more. I wasn't being mentally stimulated. I remember being bored at work and hitting the F12 key on any webpage I was on so I could inspect with DevTools. I wanted to know how it worked but I never had the confidence or know how. I thought this was something people went to university to study and that I had missed my chance.

My coding journey began in June 2019 due to curiosity, I signed up for a 4 week short course with Founders and Coders. It was an introduction to JavaScript and I knew immediately that this was what I wanted to do. The continuous growth and ever changing technology within the industry fills me with such enthusiasm and excitement. Whilst still in my previous employment I started attending meet-ups and completing courses online with Udemy, Free Code Camp and various other sites. Finally in October 2019 I enrolled in a full time Software Engineering course which covered a range of technologies. I graduated from this bootcamp in February 2020 and I still continue to further my knowledge by sttending conferences and meetups, completing more courses and creating my own projects.

My confidence has grown and now I want this more than ever. I'll do everything I can to prove to myself that I can make this transition. One day, I ultimately hope to be able to educate and influence others in this awesome community.
